Workshops

Connect with Acton-area neighbors and learn a range of cooking and baking techniques through hands-on workshops hosted in my home! Or request a private class in your kitchen for yourself, family, or friends.

Group Workshops

Join a small group of neighbors for a hands-on session in my home kitchen. We cook, we learn, we eat together.

Recent topics: Apple cider donuts, blueberry sorbet, strawberry ice cream, chocolate and marshmallow bunnies and eggs, zeppoli, cannoli, bagels, crème brûlée, gluten-free pasta, and chocolate cake!

Private Classes

Want to host a private cooking or baking class? I bring the ingredients, recipes, and instruction to your kitchen—perfect for birthdays, team events, or a night in with friends.
